Report Context: On-Time Orders, NPS, and Growth Constraints

This report uses the On-Time Orders percentage as the primary performance indicator, defined as deliveries completed within a 0–40 minute SLA. This definition was intentionally adopted as a strategic standard, both internally and in customer-facing commitments, to ensure consistency, transparency, and alignment across stakeholders.

To provide a comprehensive and executive-level view of performance, the On-Time metric is analyzed through dual-axis comparisons against key operational variables. This approach allows us to assess not only outcomes, but also the structural drivers behind performance.

The analysis is further complemented by Net Promoter Score (NPS) feedback reported by Campus General Managers, as well as a consolidated view of operational pains and strategic constraints identified by Sodexo. These factors have directly influenced campus-level scalability and growth and are critical to understanding performance beyond headline KPIs.
